Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 24, 2026  |  Last Updated: February 24, 2026

FleetMancer Labs LLC ("FleetMancer," "we," "us," or "our") operates the FleetMancer platform, a business-to-business (B2B) fleet management application for ground transportation operators. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our platform or receive communications from us.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information from authorized users of subscribing fleet operators:

  • Account information: Name, email address, phone number, and organizational role.
  • Operational data: Trip records, vehicle assignments, scheduling data, dispatch information, and rate configurations entered by your organization.
  • Driver information: Name, contact information, employment type, and vehicle assignments as entered by your organization's administrators.
  • Contact information: Names, phone numbers, and email addresses of passengers, affiliates, and other contacts entered by your organization.
  • Usage data: Log data including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and feature usage for the purpose of improving the platform.

We do not collect Social Security numbers, financial account numbers, or payment card information. Payment processing is handled entirely by our third-party payment processor (Stripe), and we do not store or have access to your payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the FleetMancer platform.
  • Send transactional communications including trip assignments, dispatch notifications, schedule changes, and system alerts via email and SMS.
  • Authenticate users and manage access permissions.
  • Respond to support requests and provide customer service.
  • Monitor and improve the performance, security, and reliability of the platform.

3. SMS/Text Messaging

If your organization enables SMS notifications through FleetMancer, you may receive text messages related to trip assignments, dispatch updates, schedule changes, and operational alerts. Message frequency varies by role and organizational usage, typically between 1 and 20 messages per business day for active drivers and dispatchers.

Consent: SMS consent is collected when an organization administrator adds your phone number to the FleetMancer platform. You will receive an initial opt-in confirmation message. Only confirmed users receive subsequent messages.

Opt-out: You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP.

Message and data rates may apply. Your mobile carrier's standard messaging and data rates apply to all SMS communications.

We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number or SMS consent with third parties for marketing purposes. SMS data, including phone numbers and consent records, will not be shared with third parties except as necessary to deliver messages (e.g., our SMS delivery provider).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your information for marketing purposes. We may share information with:

  • Service providers: Third-party services that help us operate the platform, including cloud hosting (Google Cloud Platform), database services (Neon), email delivery (Resend), SMS delivery (Twilio), error monitoring (Sentry), and payment processing (Stripe). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.
  • Your organization: Administrators within your subscribing organization can view information associated with their account, including user profiles, trip data, and operational records.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers located in the United States. We use industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption at rest for all stored data.
  • Encryption in transit (TLS) for all data transmitted between your browser and our servers.
  • Role-based access controls limiting data access to authorized users within your organization.
  • Application-level encryption for designated sensitive fields.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your organization maintains an active FleetMancer account, plus a reasonable period thereafter for legal and operational purposes. You or your organization's administrator may request deletion of your data at any time.

7. Your Rights (California Residents)

If you are a California resident, you have the right under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) to:

  • Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell personal information).
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify affected users of material changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
